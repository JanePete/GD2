Conference Speakers



Shantell Martin

Martin is avisual artist and designer who encourages her viewers to br thoughtful and intentional about what image they are putting out into the world. Her art reflects on past experiences and self-exploration.

Sam Barclay

Barclay is a Graphic Designer who has combined his experiences with dyslexia and his interest in typographic design into I Wonder What It’s Like to be Dyslexic, a book which describes and visually depicts his experience of what it’s like to read.

Sarah Hendren and Brian Glenney

Hendren and Glenney created The Accessible Icon Project, an ongoing work of design activism. It starts with a graphic icon, free for use in the public domain, and continues its work as a collaboration among people with disabilities and their allies toward a more accessible world.
